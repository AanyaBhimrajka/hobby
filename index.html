About

Hi! my Name is Aanya Bhimrajka and my favorite hobby is reading. Reading has been a huge part of my life as I started it when I was a young girl.

The reason why I love to read, is because it is so vast and imaginative. It can teach you a lot and build up many of your skills. Reading teaches you new vocabulary, it helps you build your imagination, and even sharpens your writing skills. I love reading and nothing can change that! When I read, I go into a whole new world and it is amazing! My favorite genre of reading is fiction, and my favorite sub-genre is fantasy!
